* `aligned`

$$
\begin{aligned}
f(x) &= \cos x \\\\
f\prime(x) &= -\sin x \\\\
\int_{0}^{x} f(y)dy &= \sin x
\end{aligned}
$$



```latex
\begin{aligned}
f(x) &= \cos x \\
f\prime(x) &= -\sin x \\
\int_{0}^{x} f(y)dy &= \sin x
\end{aligned}
```

* `array`

$$
  \left\\{
  \begin{array}{c}
  a_{0}+a_{1}x_{0}+...+a_{n}x_{0}^{n}=y_{0} \\\\
  a_{0}+a_{1}x_{1}+...+a_{n}x_{1}^{n}=y_{1} \\\\
  \vdots\\\\
  a_{0}+a_{1}x_{n}+...+a_{n}x_{n}^{n}=y_{n} 
  \end{array}
\right.
$$

